01 — About

I'm pursuing two degrees at once: a BBA in International Management at Ritsumeikan Asia Pacific University, and a B.Sc. in Computer Science through the University of the People. Both are equally part of who I am — I want to be an engineer who understands the business behind the product, not just the code.

I got my start with Harvard's CS50, which is what turned curiosity into commitment.

I grew up in Yangon, Myanmar, and moved through three education systems and three languages to get here. I get comfortable with unfamiliar systems fast — new languages, new codebases, new countries, doesn't matter much which.
